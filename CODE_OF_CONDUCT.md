# Code of Conduct

Employee001 is an open project. Everyone who participates — in issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other space — is expected to treat others with respect.

## The short version

Be kind. Be direct. Assume good intent. Disagree on ideas, not on people.

## What we expect

- Patient, constructive feedback on contributions
- Welcoming questions from people who are new to the project or the problem space
- Honest disagreement expressed professionally

## What we don't accept

Behaviour that makes participation unwelcoming or unsafe — including personal attacks, sustained disruption, and public disclosure of others' private information — will result in removal from project spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this standard. Reports can be sent to **office@bulldog-adv.com**. All reports are treated confidentially.
